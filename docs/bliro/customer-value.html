Revenue Methodology

Data source: HubSpot deal properties -- hs_mrr, hs_arr, hs_acv, hs_tcv, duration_in_months, rollout_potential__in_eur_arr_ -- from 175 Closed Won deals mapped to 151 companies.

Aggregation: Per company, MRR/ARR = max across deals (current run rate), TCV = sum (total contracted), rollout = max (expansion potential).

Expected ARR = adjusted_win_rate x avg_ARR_in_segment. This represents the revenue-weighted probability of winning a deal in each segment -- combining "how often we win" with "how much we win."

Best ARR targets: Segments with the highest expected ARR should be prioritized for outbound. A segment with moderate win rate but high ARR (e.g., 5K+ headcount) may be more valuable than one with high win rate but low ARR.
